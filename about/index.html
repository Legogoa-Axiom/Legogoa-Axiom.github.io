About us.

Who are we?

LegoGoa is a Goa based organization that helps young minds to learn various STEM concepts like programming and computer-aided design through the means of LEGO and robotics. While we are very dedicated towards robotics and LEGO, we place a special emphasis on generating awareness and conducting LEGO and other STEM based outreach towards the disadvantaged members of our community as well as towards the public.

Achievements of LegoGoa
